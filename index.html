How to Prevent "Slop": The Guidance Checklist

This generator automatically applies strict constraints based on presets. To guarantee premium output, remember to use these principles in your core prompt:

  • Specify Materials: Define textures like faceted polymer, crystal glass, brushed metal, or opaque plastic.
  • Define Lighting: Describe clean, directional lighting (e.g., soft top-down ambient lighting, rear backlighting).
  • Centered Silhouette: For symbols and icons, explicitly request a strong, centered silhouette on a clean background.
  • Explicitly Forbid Distortion: The generator will append constraints, but adding 'perfectly rendered', 'razor-sharp', or 'no blur/noise' helps.
