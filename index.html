Prompting Best Practices

For the best results, incorporate these elements into your core prompt:

  • Specify Materials: Mention textures like frosted glass, brushed aluminum, or matte polymer.
  • Define Lighting: Use phrases like "soft studio lighting" or "dramatic edge highlights."
  • Keep it Focused: Describe a single, clear subject rather than complex scenes.
  • Avoid Text: AI struggles with typography; focus purely on the symbol/logo mark.
