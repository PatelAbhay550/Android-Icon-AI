The Complete App Icon Size Guide (2026)

Generating the perfect image is only step one. Whether you are publishing to the Apple App Store or Google Play, your icon needs to meet strict dimensional and structural requirements. Our AI App Icon Generator handles the heavy lifting, but here is what developers need to know.

Platform Required Dimensions Format Corners
Google Play Store 512 x 512 px PNG (32-bit) Leave square (Google masks to teardrop automatically)
Apple App Store 1024 x 1024 px PNG (No alpha) Leave square (Apple applies mask)

How AI Creates Icons

Modern AI models like DALL-E 3 and Flux utilize diffusion technology. The generator automatically prepends constraints to prevent text artifacts and ensure the icon subject is centered perfectly within a square canvas.
